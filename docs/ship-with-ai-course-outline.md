## Module map

## Module 1, The credential gate
**Outcome:** students install `/ship`, run credential checks, and fix the gaps that silently kill launches.

**Lessons**
1. Why most AI launch systems fail before execution starts
2. Installing `/ship` and understanding the coordinator model
3. Running `check_local.py` across 30+ integrations
4. Fixing missing auth, expired tokens, and broken local setup
5. Building a launch-ready credential baseline

**Workbook deliverable:** credential recovery checklist + minimum viable stack by channel

### ES adaptation
**Resultado:** el alumno instala `/ship`, corre los chequeos de credenciales y corrige los huecos que rompen un lanzamiento sin avisar.

---

## Module 2, Validate stage
**Outcome:** students prove demand before building the full funnel.

**Lessons**
1. Picking the right product type: SaaS, service, course, or OSS tool
2. ICP discovery with primary-source evidence
3. Competitor and substitution research that informs messaging
4. Defining risky assumptions and kill criteria
5. Reaching a 91% confidence threshold before moving forward

**Workbook deliverable:** validation brief + confidence scorecard

### ES adaptation
**Resultado:** el alumno valida demanda con evidencia primaria antes de construir el funnel completo.

## Module 3, Strategy stage
**Outcome:** students turn research into an offer people can actually buy.

**Lessons**
1. Offer stack design for `/ship`-style launches
2. Positioning against alternatives and status quo
3. Pricing logic, value stack, and guarantee design
4. Translating strategy into one clean GTM path
5. Deciding what the agents should and should not automate

**Workbook deliverable:** positioning memo + offer stack + pricing sheet

### ES adaptation
**Resultado:** el alumno convierte investigación en una oferta clara, comprable y defendible.

## Module 4, Awareness and lead capture
**Outcome:** students build the top of funnel that feeds the system.

**Lessons**
1. Content waterfall design for pain-first distribution
2. Social, blog, and video assets that route to one CTA
3. README and install-surface CTAs for OSS tools
4. Lead magnet and landing page wiring

**Workbook deliverable:** channel plan + CTA map + lead capture assets

### ES adaptation
**Resultado:** el alumno arma una capa de awareness y captura que alimenta el funnel con una CTA consistente.

## Module 5, Nurture to closing
**Outcome:** students convert interest into purchase with a controlled sequence.

**Lessons**

2. Objection handling without generic AI copy

**Workbook deliverable:** nurture sequence + objection bank + course page outline

## Module 6, Launch day operations
**Outcome:** students run launch day like operators, not spectators.
