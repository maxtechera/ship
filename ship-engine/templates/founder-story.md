## Overview

A founder story builds trust, creates differentiation, and makes your brand human. Use across: About page, pitch decks, press kits, email intro, LinkedIn, podcast bios.

**The core structure:** Origin → Problem lived → Insight → What you built → Mission → Social proof

## Format Variants

| Format | Length | Where to use |
|--------|--------|--------------|
| **Tweet / Bio** | 1–2 sentences | Twitter, LinkedIn headline |
| **Short** | 1 paragraph (100–150 words) | Email footer, podcast intro |
| **Medium** | 3–5 paragraphs (300–500 words) | About page, press kit |
| **Long / Video** | 600–1000 words | Deep-dive article, pitch deck narrative |

---

## Writing Tips

- **Specific > Generic**: "Lost $40k" beats "lost a lot of money"
- **Present tense** for mission/product; past tense for story
- **Avoid jargon** — write for a smart non-technical reader
- **One protagonist** — this is YOUR story, not "the team's"
- **End with a call to action** when using on landing pages: "Want to see what we built? →"
