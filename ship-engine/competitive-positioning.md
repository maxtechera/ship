## Top 3 Objections + Rebuttals

### Objection 1: "Xero/QuickBooks already does this"

**When you hear it**: Prospect has used international SaaS before, or is comparing based on feature volume.

**Rebuttal**:
> "Xero and QuickBooks are excellent for international businesses — but neither integrates with DGI, BPS, or MTSS. That means if you use them in Uruguay, you *still* need a local accountant to handle compliance separately. Conta.uy eliminates that double cost. You get one tool, built for Uruguay, at a fraction of the price."

**Supporting facts**:
- Xero has 0 DGI integration, 0 BPS payroll, 0 CFE emission
- QuickBooks has no Spanish-first support for Uruguay
- Both cost 4–10x more per month in UYU

---

### Objection 2: "My accountant handles all of this"

**When you hear it**: Prospect is used to delegating everything to a human accountant.

**Rebuttal**:
> "That's great — and Conta.uy doesn't replace your accountant, it makes them faster. Most accountants spend hours on manual data entry and DGI submissions. With Conta.uy, the CFE emissions, DGI reports, and BPS declarations are automated. Your accountant gets to focus on advice, not data entry. And you get real-time visibility into your numbers."

**Supporting facts**:
- Average accountant cost: 8,000–25,000 UYU/mo vs. Conta.uy at ~990–2,490 UYU/mo
- Manual accountant onboarding: 1–4 weeks; Conta.uy: < 1 hour
- Self-serve gives founders real-time financial visibility

---

### Objection 3: "Alegra works for LATAM — isn't that good enough for Uruguay?"

**When you hear it**: Prospect has heard of Alegra or used it in another LATAM country.

**Rebuttal**:
> "Alegra is a solid tool for Colombia, Argentina, and other LATAM markets — but Uruguay has a unique compliance stack: DGI e-invoicing (CFE), BPS social security payroll, MTSS labor compliance, and BPS GAFI. Alegra supports none of these natively. You'd need workarounds and local add-ons, which defeats the point. Conta.uy was built for Uruguay *specifically*."

**Supporting facts**:
- Alegra: no DGI, no BPS, no MTSS, no CFE — zero Uruguay compliance modules
- Alegra pricing is 1.8–4.2x more than Conta.uy for less Uruguay coverage
- "LATAM-compatible" ≠ "Uruguay-compliant"

## Feature Gaps to Watch

These are potential areas where competitors *could* build and erode Conta.uy's moat. Monitor quarterly.

| Gap / Threat | Who Might Build It | Timeline Risk | Our Response |
|---|---|---|---|
| **DGI API integration** | Alegra (if they target UY) | 12–18 months | Deepen DGI automation; build migration tools before they ship |
| **BPS payroll module** | Local Uruguayan SaaS (new entrant) | 6–12 months | Lock in brand/trust early with content marketing; become the default name for "contabilidad Uruguay" |
| **WhatsApp CRM** | Any LATAM accounting SaaS + Twilio | 6–12 months | Expand WhatsApp flows beyond support to invoicing, payment reminders |
| **UYU-native pricing** | Xero (via LATAM expansion) | 24+ months | Not a near-term risk; Xero is slow to localize |
| **AI-assisted tax filing** | Any incumbent with AI investment | 12–24 months | Ship AI filing suggestions before AI-first startups commoditize it |
| **Accountant partnership marketplace** | Freelancer platforms (e.g. Workana) | 12–18 months | Build accountant partner network proactively |
