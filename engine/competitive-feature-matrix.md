## Key Takeaways

1. **Conta.uy is the only SaaS with native DGI + BPS + MTSS + CFE coverage** — competitors have zero Uruguay-specific compliance.
2. **Price gap is 4–10x** — Conta.uy is the most affordable option for Uruguay-specific compliance.
3. **Alegra is the closest LATAM competitor** but has no DGI or BPS integration — significant moat.
4. **Local accountants have deep knowledge but no self-serve, no mobile, slow and expensive**.
5. **WhatsApp-native support** is a unique Conta.uy differentiator in a WhatsApp-dominant market.
