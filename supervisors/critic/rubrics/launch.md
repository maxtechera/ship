# Critic Rubric — Launch (Stage 7)

Applies to: launch announcement, launch sequence, public post, press/outreach

## Launch Announcement Checks

| # | Check | PASS criteria | REVISE trigger |
|---|-------|--------------|----------------|
| 1 | Primary artifact published | Live URL or platform post link present | Not published |
| 2 | Copy from VoC | Announcement uses ≥1 verbatim or near-verbatim pain phrase from VoC bank | Generic or invented language |
| 3 | Copy humanized | No AI cadence; reads like a human founder wrote it | AI-sounding phrases detected |
| 4 | CTA present | Clear single CTA (link, signup, buy) | No CTA or multiple conflicting CTAs |
| 5 | UTMs on links | All links include UTM params (source, medium, campaign) | Missing UTMs |
| 6 | Offer matches closing | Pricing and offer wording matches Closing output exactly | Mismatch with closing deliverables |

## Launch Sequence Checks

| # | Check | PASS criteria | REVISE trigger |
|---|-------|--------------|----------------|
| 7 | Day 0 email sent | Launch email delivered to full list, confirmed | Not sent or not confirmed |
| 8 | Social posts scheduled | All platforms from strategy channel plan have Day 0 post scheduled | Any priority channel missing |
| 9 | Tracking firing | Conversion events live in GA4 on launch day | Events not confirmed |
| 10 | Rollback ready | Rollback plan is documented and accessible | Missing rollback plan |

## REVISE output required
List each failing check + specific fix instruction.
