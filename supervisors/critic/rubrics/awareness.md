# Critic Rubric — Awareness (Stage 5A)

Applies to: landing page, blog pack, social pack, image pack, b-roll manifest

## Landing Page Checks

| # | Check | PASS criteria | REVISE trigger |
|---|-------|--------------|----------------|
| 1 | Live URL | URL returns 200, page loads | Broken link / not deployed |
| 2 | Hero copy from VoC | Headline uses ≥1 verbatim or near-verbatim pain phrase from VoC bank | Invented headline unrelated to VoC |
| 3 | Copy humanized | No AI-sounding phrases (e.g., "delve", "unlock the power of", "comprehensive solution") | Clear AI-generated cadence detected |
| 4 | Mobile screenshot | Mobile screenshot attached or linked | Missing |
| 5 | Desktop screenshot | Desktop screenshot attached or linked | Missing |
| 6 | OG tags | OG title, description, image present (verify via share preview or source) | Missing OG tags |
| 7 | CTA present | Clear primary CTA above the fold | No CTA |

## Blog Pack Checks

| # | Check | PASS criteria | REVISE trigger |
|---|-------|--------------|----------------|
| 8 | Articles linked | ≥1 article with PR-ready frontmatter linked | No articles |
| 9 | Word count | Each article ≥1500 words | Any article <1500 words |
| 10 | Copy humanized | No AI-sounding phrases | AI cadence detected |
| 11 | Keywords present | ≥5 long-tail keywords per article woven naturally | Keywords stuffed or absent |
| 12 | Internal links | ≥2 internal links per article (to other articles or landing page) | No internal links |

## Social Pack Checks

| # | Check | PASS criteria | REVISE trigger |
|---|-------|--------------|----------------|
| 13 | At least 2 platforms | Content for ≥2 platforms | Only 1 platform |
| 14 | Platform formatting | Each platform's content follows correct format (thread for X, carousel for IG, etc.) | Wrong format |
| 15 | UTM links | CTAs in social posts include UTM params | Missing UTMs |
| 16 | Copy humanized | Platform-appropriate voice; no AI cadence | AI cadence detected |
