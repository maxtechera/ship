# Critic Rubric — Nurture (Stage 5C)

Applies to: email sequence, content drip schedule

## Email Sequence Checks

| # | Check | PASS criteria | REVISE trigger |
|---|-------|--------------|----------------|
| 1 | ≥5 emails | At least 5 complete emails (subject + body + CTA) | <5 emails |
| 2 | Day 0 delivers promise | Email #1 delivers the lead magnet / promised value | Email #1 is generic welcome only |
| 3 | Copy humanized | No AI cadence, written in brand voice | AI cadence detected |
| 4 | VoC language used | Pain language from ICP VoC bank woven into copy | Generic pain language |
| 5 | CTAs stage-appropriate | Days 0-3 → activate; Days 5-10 → convert; Day 14 → last call | Wrong CTA for sequence stage |
| 6 | Live in provider | Automation imported and active in email provider | Not imported |
| 7 | Trigger configured | Automation fires when subscriber joins lead capture group | Trigger not set |
| 8 | Test email verified | Test email sent + delivery confirmed + links tracked | Not tested |

## Content Drip Checks

| # | Check | PASS criteria | REVISE trigger |
|---|-------|--------------|----------------|
| 9 | schedule.csv exists | File with day/platform/copy/image columns linked | Missing |
| 10 | ≥7 days planned | At least 7 days of posts | <7 days |
| 11 | Platform adapted | Posts are platform-appropriate (not one-size-fits-all) | All same text across platforms |
